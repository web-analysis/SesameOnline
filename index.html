How it works?

It will help you upload your file to openload, and then it will
receive your download link from openload. After setting a ticket
for your download link, your download link will be saved
automatically. Next time just use the ticket to download your file.


What kinds of files and what's the maximum file size can you upload?

You should refer to openload&FAQ.


how long your files will be saved?

No! your file are saved at least 30 days on openload. This program
only saves your download link about 10 days. If you still want to
download after 10 days, you should remember download link yourself.


Delete your ticket?

You don't need. It will be automatically deleted after 10 days.
